Privacy Policy

This add-on is designed to minimize data access and does not collect, sell, or share personal user data.

Google Sheets access. The add-on only operates within the currently active Google Sheets™ spreadsheet where the user invokes the provided custom functions. The developer does not access other spreadsheets outside the user's explicit actions.

External data source (Yahoo Finance™). When a user calls a function (for example with a ticker symbol), the add-on retrieves public market data from Yahoo Finance™. Requests are performed via secure HTTPS/TLS connections. The information sent to Yahoo Finance™ is limited to what is necessary to fulfill the request (e.g., the ticker symbol and request parameters).

No permanent storage of spreadsheet contents. We do not store, retain, or persist users' spreadsheet contents, files, or personal information on external servers or databases. Data is processed only to provide the requested functionality.

Temporary caching (performance). To reduce repeated requests and improve performance, the add-on may use Google Apps Script's caching mechanisms to temporarily cache Yahoo Finance™ responses. This cache is time-limited and expires automatically. It is used solely for performance and reliability, and is not used for profiling or advertising.

Data protection & security measures. All transmissions to external services are encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Access to Google services is handled through Googl's OAuth framework. We apply access controls to limit data handling to the minimum necessary for the user-requested features.

Data sharing. We do not sell user data and we do not share Google user data with third parties. The add-on only communicates with Yahoo Finance™ to retrieve public financial information requested by the user.

Data retention and deletion. Because we do not permanently store personal or spreadsheet data, there is no long-term retention of such data by the developer. Any temporary cached data expires automatically according to the cache duration.

No advertising or marketing use. The developer does not use data for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.
