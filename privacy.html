Privacy Policy

This add-on does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The add-on only accesses the currently active Google Sheets™ spreadsheet in which the user invokes the provided custom functions. No other spreadsheets are accessed.

Public financial data is retrieved from Yahoo Finance™ via secure HTTPS requests.

No personal information is sent to or stored by the developer.

The developer does not use the data for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.
